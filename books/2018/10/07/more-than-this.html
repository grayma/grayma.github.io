More Than This

Oct 7, 2018

John Green’s recommendation of this book isn’t an overstatement. More Than This is a fantastic fiction book that has some delightful twists throughout. I have a similar feeling towards this book as I did towards Ready Player One: it’s not a forced young-adult or teenage perspective, but a realistic read inside the mind of a young character. Also, I’m a sucker for low word-per-page density and this satisfied that :).
